The Benefit Of Online
Classes For The ELDT Course

The Entry-Level Driver Training mandates require new entry-level drivers to complete Theory Instructions and Behind-The-Wheel training from a certified training provider (i.e. a truck driving school certified by FMCSA) in order to be eligible to get their Commercial Driver's License.

In this context, online classes for Theory Instructions become an easy option for students to complete an integral part of their training before joining a driving school to complete Behind-The Wheel instructions and give the skills test for obtaining a Class A or Class B CDL. This way, the students can get their CDL quickly while preparing from their homes using their smartphones.
