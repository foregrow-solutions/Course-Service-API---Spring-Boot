The Objective of ELDT Inc As an ELDT
Online Training Provider

The goal of ELDT Inc is to give our students a safe learning space where they can study at a pace that suits them with detailed course curriculum and video lectures, so that they develop a strong foundation for Theory Instructions as per the ELDT mandates. We want to build their driving knowledge with a solid base, providing them with the skills needed to be a responsible commercial motor vehicle driver.

ELDT Inc's rationale is rooted in the belief that education should be engaging, fun and comprehensible, and centered on students' requirements. The ELDT course has been made with the assistance of industry experts possessing profound knowledge in driver training instructions. Their guidance has produced a learning platform that delivers on the needs of driver-trainees to the fullest - a platform that caters to the learning requirements of students in the simplest, graspable manner but never compromises on the quality of the content they study.

ELDT Inc is a certified service provider - our ELDT course is approved by the FMCSA.
